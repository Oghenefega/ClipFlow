Preview Overview. The preview shows the video and it shows the subtitles that are being said, the transcript of the video, which also is the subtitle. It shows that in whatever selected font template or font style chosen. It also shows the caption. The caption is different from the subtitle. The caption is the embedded "title of the video". What is meant to be eye-catching to the viewer? That can be selected and certain things can be changed. Maybe the font size, the color, the casing of the text, the font itself can be changed. The preview also has the ability to zoom in and out of the preview just to make what is being shown on the preview screen larger or smaller so you can fit to size, zoom in, or zoom out.
